# Strategy Prompt Templates

Copy-paste these prompts into any agent with AletaIndex connected.

These are just starting points — feel free to ask in your own words. The agent understands natural language questions about narratives, sentiment, and portfolio risk. You're not limited to the templates below.

---

## Daily Briefing

```
Give me a morning briefing on NVDA and TSLA for the last 7 days.
For each ticker: what are the dominant narratives, what's the overall
sentiment trend, and are there any narratives gaining momentum or fading?
```

---

## Price-Sentiment Divergence

```
For TSLA, look at the last 14 days of narratives and sentiment.
Is there any divergence between the sentiment trend and price movement?
What might that signal about how the market is pricing the narrative risk?
```

---

## Tail Risk Early Warning

```
For NVDA over the last 30 days, identify any narratives with low article
count but strongly negative sentiment. These could be early-stage risks
before they reach mainstream coverage. How serious do they look?
```

---

## Conviction Check

```
I'm long AAPL. Pull the last 30 days of narratives.
Flag any narratives that could challenge my thesis — especially ones
gaining momentum or with worsening sentiment. Rank them by threat level.
```

---

## Sentiment Shift Attribution

```
For TSLA, find any day in the last 30 days where sentiment shifted
sharply in either direction. What narratives drove that shift,
and does it look like a signal or noise?
```

---

## Portfolio Narrative Risk

```
Analyze narrative risk across my portfolio:
NVDA 30%, AAPL 25%, TSLA 20%, MSFT 25%

Which macro narratives create concentrated exposure across multiple
holdings? Which positions carry the most narrative-driven downside risk?
```

---

## Cross-Ticker Divergence

```
Compare the narrative landscape for NVDA and AMD over the last 30 days.
Where are they aligned, and where are they diverging?
What does the divergence tell you about how the market is differentiating them?
```

---

## What Actually Happened This Week

```
For TSLA, pull the last 7 days of narratives.
Which narratives are brand new this week — just emerged?
Which are ongoing threads that got fresh coverage this week?
For the most active ones, which specific days had the most articles,
and what triggered the spike?
```

---

## Sector Sweep

```
Get a quick narrative snapshot for:
TSLA, NVDA, AAPL, MSFT, AMZN, GOOGL, META, AMD, NFLX, JPM

For each: dominant narrative, sentiment label, article count.
Which ticker has the most concerning narrative picture right now, and why?
```
